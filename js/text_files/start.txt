Welcome to ScriptUNC!<br>
In this game, you will learn how to program using Javascript!<br>
To help you get started, here are a few controls:<br>
- Click anywhere on screen to move to that position.<br>
- Click and drag anywhere to have control of the camera.<br>
- Click on the mini map in the top left corner to recenter the screen on Rameses!<br>
<br>
Try exploring the map for a bit, and to continue, click on the prompt:<br>
"scriptUNC.console ~ >" in the top left corner of the console.<br>
