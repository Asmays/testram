Welcome to ScriptUNC!\n
In this game, you will learn how to program using Javascript!\n
To help you get started, here are a few controls:\n
- Click anywhere on screen to move to that position.\n
- Click and drag anywhere to have control of the camera.\n
- Click on the mini map in the top left corner to recenter the screen on Rameses!\n
\n
Try exploring the map for a bit, and to continue, click on the prompt:\n
"scriptUNC.console ~ >" in the top left corner of the console.\n
